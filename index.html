About Us

Why Choose Us

Quixxo is a location-based safety website designed to enhance personal safety by utilizing geographic information. It includes features like real-time location tracking and, emergency alerts.


Users can share their location with trusted contacts, receive notifications in case of emergencies, and access local safety information. Overall, it aims to leverage location data to provide users with a heightened sense of security and quick response in case of potential threats or emergencies.
